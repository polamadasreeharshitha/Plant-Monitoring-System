About Project


Our project is plant monitoring system.It helps in effective water management through automation of a plant.The microcontroller we have used is raspberry pi 3 and we have used 4 different sensors i.e DHT11(temperture and humidity sensor),LM393(raindop sensor),(soil moisture sensor)HCSR04 (Ultrasonic sensor).Also there is a motor to pump the water.The project solely revolves around the idea that if the moiture content of the plant is low the motor must switch on automatically and pump enough water.
